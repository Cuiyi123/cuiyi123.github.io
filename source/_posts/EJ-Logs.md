### <font color=Blue>Log[1]</font>
I usually take the subway to my working company in the morning. Whenever commuting from A to B every day, I always notice that most of the passengers on the subway look at their mobile phones being held on their hands, with their heads down and their eyes fixing on them. Maybe they are browsing the messages on Friends' Circle, or maybe they are watching Headline News or maybe they are watching TV shows. No one knows whether doing these things is boring or it was simply a way of killing time. Sometimes I feel bored and time-wasting after surfing Headline News for a while on my mobile. Some may say that everyone is doing this as they do when taking the subway.
